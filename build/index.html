Welcome to Flipcon

Join us on December 5th to play games and raise money in support of the two Democratic candidates in the Georgia run-off election.

How it works is simple:

  • GMs schedule games
  • Players make a donation to the campaigns of either Jon Ossoff or Raphael Warnock (links below)
  • Players show proof of the donation to the GM, which secures them a spot at the (virtual) table

The GM can set the minimum donation required to secure a seat, but recommend any amount. Some GMs may also offer in-game perks for donations of specific amounts; see the game description for details.

Let's have fun, and flip the Senate!

Important Legal Stuff

  • You must be a U.S. citizen or lawfully admitted permanent resident (i.e., green card holder).
  • This contribution is made from your own funds, and funds are not being provided to you by another person or entity for the purpose of making this contribution. You must be at least eighteen years old.
  • You are not a federal contractor.
  • You are making this contribution with your own personal credit card and not with a corporate or business credit card or a card issued to another person.
  • You are not a registered Federal lobbyist.
  • You are not a registered foreign agent.
