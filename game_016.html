Lights In the Sky - DCC / MCC RPG
Your PCs are hired by the mutants who live outside the walls of St. Angelique to investigate the strange lights in the sky over their shantytown hovels as well as the disappearance of some of their own. This is a mashup of DCC RPG & MCC RPG rules and classes, so expect Dwarves, Mutants, Elves, Manimals, Warriors, Wizards, Shamans, etc.
