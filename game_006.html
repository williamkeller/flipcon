The Cuthbert Lovelace Affair
Several eligible young ladies are dispatched to meet the handsome, wealthy and eccentric bachelor Cuthbert Lovelace. Little did they dream that the Lovelace name (and fortune) comes with so much dangerous baggage! And what a way to find out... stranded at the Lovelace manor home, cut off from the rest of the world for at least 3 days.
This is a Gothic Horror game, played with a super light version of GUMSHOE rules. Minimal combat. Expect mystery, creepy castles, spider webs, secret passages, searching crypts by candlelight, etc.
