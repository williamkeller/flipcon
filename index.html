Welcome to Flipcon

Join us on December 5th to play games and raise money in support of the two Democrat candidates in the Georgia run-off election.

How it works is simple:

  • GMs schedule games
  • Players make a donation to the campaigns of either Jon Ossoff or Raphael Warnock (links below)
  • Players show proof of the donation to the GM, which secures them a spot at the (virtual) table

The GM can set the minimum donation required to secure a seat, but recommend any amount. Some GMs may also offer in-game perks for donations of specific amounts; see the game description for details.
